Why Clinics Are Different

Clinic Data Requires a Higher Standard of Protection

A patient's records, scanned documents, treatment photographs, and consent forms are irreplaceable. A clinic that loses this data faces disruption to patient care, difficulty reconstructing records, and significant operational risk.

Most clinics store data across a practice management system, shared folders, individual staff computers, and cloud platforms — often without a coordinated backup strategy.

CTS360 designs a structured, monitored hybrid backup solution that gives your clinic fast local access to files, secure off-site cloud protection, and the confidence of knowing your data can actually be restored when needed.

Security Controls

Clinic-Level Access Control & Security

Access to patient data is configured by role — so each member of your team sees only what they need to do their job.

Role-Based Access

Separate access profiles for doctors, nurses, front desk, finance, and clinic management. Each role sees only what is relevant to their function.

Encryption & MFA

Data encrypted at rest and in transit. Multi-factor authentication available for remote access and cloud platforms, reducing unauthorised access risk.

Firewall & Endpoint Security

Business firewall protecting the clinic network, with endpoint security deployed across all staff computers — reducing ransomware and malware risk.

Backup Monitoring & Alerts

CTS360 monitors backup jobs daily. Failed backups trigger alerts and are investigated — so you are not left with a backup gap you discover too late.

Staff Departure Controls

When a staff member leaves, we handle immediate account suspension and access removal — preventing ex-employees from accessing clinic systems or patient data.

Recovery Testing

Periodic test restores verify that your backup data can actually be recovered — giving you confidence before a real incident occurs, not during one.

Managed Service

NAS Health Monitoring

Backup Job Monitoring

Remote helpdesk support for clinic staff — resolving day-to-day IT issues without waiting for a technician to arrive on-site.
