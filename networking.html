Services

Our Networking Services

End-to-end network design, installation, and ongoing support for offices of all sizes.

Network Design

We design the right architecture for your office — identifying access point locations, switch placement, and cabling routes before a single cable is run.

Business Wi-Fi

Enterprise-grade wireless access points installed for complete, consistent coverage — no dead zones, no overcrowded channels, no consumer-grade shortcuts.

Firewall Deployment

Business firewall installation and configuration — protecting your network from external threats while keeping internal traffic fast and reliable.

Managed Switches

Installation and configuration of managed network switches — providing stable wired connectivity for workstations, printers, NAS devices, and IP phones.

Guest Wi-Fi & VLANs

Separate your guest, staff, and device networks — keeping visitor traffic isolated from your business systems, and departmental data properly segmented.

VPN & Remote Access

Secure VPN configuration enabling staff to access office systems safely from home or while travelling — without compromising network security.

Multi-Site Connectivity

Network links between your Singapore and Malaysia offices — enabling shared file access, unified communication, and consistent security policies.

Network Monitoring

Ongoing monitoring of network performance and availability — with proactive alerts and rapid response when an issue is detected.

Network Documentation

Every installation is documented — IP addressing, device inventory, cable layout, and configuration notes — so troubleshooting and future changes are straightforward.
