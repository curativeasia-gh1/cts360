How CTS360 Supports Your Xero Environment

Initial Technical Setup

Xero account setup, organisation settings, chart of accounts review with your accountant, and initial configuration to match your business structure.

User Access & Roles

User account setup, role and permission configuration so staff have access to the functions they need without exposing sensitive financial data unnecessarily.

Device & Browser Setup

Configuration of laptops, desktops, and browsers for Xero access. Device setup for finance team members, including bookmarks, email workflow, and document storage.

Document & Workflow Setup

Invoice template configuration, email sending workflow, document storage organisation, and file naming conventions that work with your accounting process.

Bank Feed Coordination

Assistance coordinating bank feed connection with your bank and accountant. Confirmation testing once the connection is established.

Application Integration

Technical setup and coordination for connecting Xero with compatible third-party applications such as inventory, CRM, or payment platforms.
