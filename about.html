Who We Are

Built for the Businesses That Build Everything Else

CTS360 – Curative Tech Services is an IT solutions provider focused exclusively on small and medium-sized enterprises. We understand that most SMEs don't have the resources for a large internal IT department, and that the alternative — calling different vendors for every different problem — is frustrating, expensive, and unreliable.

We built CTS360 to solve that problem. One team, one point of contact, covering everything from your office network and laptop fleet to your backup system and cloud platforms — across Singapore and Malaysia.

Our approach is straightforward: understand your business first, recommend what you actually need, implement it cleanly, and support it properly. No upselling, no unnecessary complexity, and no disappearing after the installation is done.
