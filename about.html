Who We Are

CTS360 — Curative Tech Services

CTS360 (Curative Tech Services) is an IT solutions provider focused on practical technology for small and medium-sized enterprises in Singapore and Malaysia.

Our services cover managed IT support, office networking, cloud business applications, Synology NAS with daily cloud backup, Brother printers, professional labelling systems, laptops, and business hardware.

Rather than recommending unnecessary complexity, we help customers select and implement solutions that are functional, manageable, and suitable for their operations — and we support them after implementation.
