Service Coverage

What Your Managed IT Plan Covers

A comprehensive set of services that keeps your team productive and your technology running reliably.

Remote Helpdesk

Fast remote support for your team's day-to-day IT issues — software, account access, connectivity, and more — resolved without waiting for a site visit.

On-Site Support

Scheduled and emergency visits to your office for hardware issues, setup tasks, and anything that requires a technician on-site.

Endpoint Monitoring

Proactive monitoring of laptops, desktops, and servers — catching performance issues, disk warnings, and security alerts before they become problems.

Cybersecurity & Antivirus

Endpoint protection deployment and management, software update enforcement, and security configuration across all managed devices.

M365 / Google Workspace Admin

User account management, licence administration, email configuration, Teams/Meet setup, SharePoint/Drive management, and productivity app support.

Vendor & Warranty Management

We handle warranty claims, vendor escalations, ISP coordination, and device replacement — so your team doesn't spend time on tech admin.

User Onboarding & Offboarding

New joiner device setup, account creation, and software installation. Full offboarding and access removal when staff leave — protecting your business data.

IT Asset Management

Tracking of all managed devices — purchase dates, warranty expiry, software licences, and replacement planning — so nothing gets overlooked.

Network & Backup Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of your network and backup systems — with alerts and prompt resolution when issues are detected.
