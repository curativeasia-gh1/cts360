What We Cover

Managed IT Services Include

Remote Helpdesk

Fast remote support for day-to-day IT issues — software problems, email issues, printer faults, access resets, and application errors resolved without waiting for an on-site visit.

On-Site Support

Scheduled or priority on-site visits for hardware issues, new equipment setup, office changes, and situations that cannot be resolved remotely.

Endpoint Monitoring

Monitoring of laptops, desktops, and devices to detect problems early. Antivirus management and software updates applied on a managed schedule.

Network & Backup Monitoring

Network health monitoring, Synology NAS monitoring, backup job monitoring, and failed-backup alerts so you always know your data is protected.

User Lifecycle Management

New employee onboarding — account creation, device setup, email configuration, and application access. Employee exit — access removal across all systems and applications.

Cloud Application Admin

Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace administration — licences, shared mailboxes, Teams, OneDrive, SharePoint, permissions, and user account management.
